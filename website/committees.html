Web Development

The ACM Web Development Team is a hard working and growing team in the ACM. Our goal is to teach the fundumentals of Web Development and that the Web Development is more than just building websites. In today's world almost everythng you use is some form of web application. Understanding Web Development is extremely important because it is the foundation of todays tech industry. While our team does build web sites on the side as favors to other clubs, we also strive to build powerful web applications that interact with backends and databases in order to help better prepare ourselves for the real world and better educate oursleves in an area of the field of computer science. One of our major current projects is the Rowan ACM website, which we are collaborating with Mobile App Development with. If you have any questions contact Chris Pryer @ pryerc1@students.rowan.edu

Mobile App Development

Rowan University Mobile Application Development (R.U. M.A.D.) is focused on building innovative mobile applications for iOS and Android. As more people are accessing the internet exclusively on mobile devices, it is important for Computer Science students to transition as well. We are currently building an app for Rowan ACM. It will allow members to receive real time information about the club and help other members. We are collaborating with the Web Development Team to integrate the ACM app with the website. If you're not on slack please email Tyler Carberry @ carberryt9@students.rowan.edu. For questions and/or more info, please contact John Saunders @ saundersj0@students.rowan.edu.
