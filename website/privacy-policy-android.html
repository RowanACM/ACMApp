Privacy Policy

Rowan ACM
Last updated on March 12th, 2017

Our Privacy Policy sets out how we collect, use and disclose non personal information we have gathered about you.

What information do we collect from you?
Rowan ACM collects general usage information to improve the app. This includes crash reporting and usage info via Firebase Analytics.

Google Account Permission
When you sign in to your Google Account, you provide Rowan ACM with access to your full name and email address. This is used for the meeting's attendance and to subscribe you to the club's email list.

Disclosure of Personal Information
We will not sell, trade, rent or disclose any of the personal information you provide to us for any reason, without your prior consent. Otherwise, your Personal Information will only be disclosed where required or allowable by law.
