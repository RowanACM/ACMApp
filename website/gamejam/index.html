ACM Game Jam

Join us in friendly competition to make a game from scratch in just 48 hours! Make any game you want in any engine or genre, based off a secret theme that will be announced at the start of the jam.

  • Sign Up

Details

Location: Robinson 201a/b

Opening Ceremony: Friday November 2nd 4-6 PM. Find out the theme and pick people to work with, or work by yourself. There will be pizza!

Midevent: Break off and find somewhere to work with your team.

Closing Ceremony: Sunday November 4th 4-6 PM. Meet back to submit your games and find out the winners. There will be pizza again! There may also be Jam and Toast.
